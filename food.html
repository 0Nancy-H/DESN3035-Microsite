Stuck looking for places to eat in LA?

No problem—I've got you! I’ve put together a list of places I’ve tried and would recommend.
Just click the red location icon, and it will take you directly to the restaurant's map location.


Marugame Udon

If you’re in search of an affordable and delicious bowl of udon, Marugame in the heart of Little Tokyo is the spot! They serve a variety of handcrafted udon dishes, along with tempting appetizers like tempura to complement your meal.

In and Out

When you visit Los Angeles, you can't miss the chance to grab In-N-Out burgers at least once during your trip! Be sure to try their animal-style fries—they're a must! It’s a fantastic option for a quick meal on the go.

Phoholic

Los Angeles has a vibrant Vietnamese community, and you must visit Phoholic while you’re here. Their famous broth is bursting with flavor, and they provide a diverse selection of pho options to enjoy.

Vox Kitchen

Vox Kitchen in Fountain Valley showcases a diverse fusion of Asian-inspired dishes that seamlessly combine traditional flavors with modern techniques. Emphasizing fresh ingredients, their menu features a variety of options, from delicious bao buns to inventive rice bowls, making it a must-visit for any food lover.

Brodard Restaurant

A trip to Brodard is a must, known for its famous spring rolls and a diverse range of Vietnamese dishes. It's an excellent option for newcomers to Vietnamese cuisine as well as seasoned enthusiasts. Plus, if you’re still craving something sweet after your meal, they have a wonderful selection of desserts to choose from!

Quán Mii Restaurant

Located in Bolsa, Quan Mii is a hidden gem for enjoying crispy banh xeo. This charming restaurant offers a delightful selection of Vietnamese dishes, making it a perfect spot for those seeking authentic flavors.
