Still not full? Theres always room for dessert!

I’ve put together a list of dessert spots I've enjoyed and would recommend.
Just click the red location icon to access each restaurant's map location directly.

Melo Melo

If you’re a coconut enthusiast, this is the place for you! Melo Melo presents an array of delicious coconut desserts served in eco-friendly glass jars. The menu boasts unique flavors and combinations, catering to a variety of tastes and preferences.

Sul & Beans

Sul & Beans showcases the traditional Korean shaved ice known as bingsu. With a wide selection of flavors and generous portions ideal for sharing, it’s a delightful spot for dessert. Alongside bingsu, they also offer a variety of drinks and flavored toast, making it a fantastic destination for a sweet treat.

Kem Mmm Gelato

Kem mm Gelato is renowned for its rich and creamy gelato crafted from premium ingredients, including unique flavors like durian. When you visit, be sure to try their smash avocado dessert, which is a must-have!

Somi Somi

Somi Somi is a beloved dessert spot for all ages, renowned for its iconic taiyaki—fish-shaped cakes filled with various sweet fillings and topped with creamy ice cream and delicious toppings. This unique treat offers a delightful combination of warm, soft cake and cool, smooth ice cream, making it a favorite among dessert lovers.
